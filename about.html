Our Story

Saffron & Sage started in 2020 with a simple dream: to create a cozy place where people feel at home. What began as a small coffee corner has grown into a warm community cafe loved by families and friends.

We believe great food brings people together. That's why every dish is made fresh with local ingredients and a whole lot of love.
